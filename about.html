Trade Suppliers

We are always looking for new and innovative products and services that meet our customer’s and business needs. If you have a product or service that you want
to sell at any of our 7-Eleven stores, you may contact our head office to set an appointment with
our Marketing department who will get you in touch with category managers handling your product or service.

Non-Trade Suppliers

For parties wishing to be listed as non-trade goods and services suppliers, kindly download and accomplish the 3 documents found below.

Please submit the accomplished forms and complete requirements to:

Jennifer Valdez
Procurement Planning Specialist
Philippine Seven Corporation
11th Floor (Room 118) Procurement Division
Columbia Tower, Ortigas Ave., Mandaluyong City
705-5381

Definition:

Non-trade Goods – refer to capital and non-capital expenditures acquired for use in the operational business but not intended for re-sale to customers.

Non-trade Services – refer to construction works, security services, janitorial services, pest control management, preventive maintenance and repairs, and other
services necessary for the usual operation of the business.
